**Check 1 — Header band and logo (D-02, D-04):**
1. Look at the top of the page — a colored header band should span the full viewport width

**Check 2 — Color palette (D-01, D-03):**
1. Click "Transform Recipe" without entering anything (or paste any text)
2. Verify: the submit button is warm green (not black/zinc-900)
3. Verify: any focus rings that appear are green (not black)

**Check 3 — Diet pill selected state (D-06):**
1. Click one or two diet pills (e.g., "Vegan", "Keto")
2. Verify: selected pills have a solid warm-green fill with white text — they look clearly ON
3. Verify: unselected pills look clearly different (white/light background)
4. The "glance test": without reading the labels, can you immediately tell which pills are selected? If yes, PASS.

**Check 4 — Diet pill info popovers (D-07):**
1. Click the small ⓘ icon next to a diet pill label (e.g., next to "Vegan")
2. Verify: a popover opens (not a tooltip — it should stay open after you move the mouse)
3. Verify: the popover has a solid white/opaque background (no transparency or blur artifacts)
4. Verify: clicking outside the popover closes it
5. Verify: the popover text matches the diet description

**Check 5 — Typography (D-09, D-10):**
To verify typography you need a transformed recipe. Paste a simple recipe, select a diet, transform:

1. Paste: "Pasta with butter, flour, and egg. Cook pasta. Melt butter. Serve."
2. Select any diet constraint and click "Transform Recipe"
3. After transformation: look at the recipe card
4. Verify: "Ingredients" and "Instructions" section headings appear BOLD (heavier than surrounding text)
5. Verify: instruction steps have slightly more line spacing than a tight packed list (leading-relaxed)

**Check 6 — Responsive page padding (D-08):**
1. At desktop width (> 1024px): the page content should have comfortable padding from viewport edges
2. Resize browser to approximately 700px wide (tablet): slightly less padding, still looks good
3. Resize to mobile (< 640px): minimal 16px (px-4) side padding; content fills most of the viewport
4. No content should touch viewport edges at any width

**Check 7 — Focus rings (D-13):**
1. Press Tab key to cycle through interactive elements on the page
2. Verify: diet pill buttons show a visible ring when focused (not just an outline color change)
3. Verify: ServingStepper +/- buttons show a visible ring when focused
4. Verify: the textarea shows a visible ring when focused via keyboard (Tab)
5. Click on a pill, then Tab away — ring should disappear on mouse click (focus-visible behavior)
